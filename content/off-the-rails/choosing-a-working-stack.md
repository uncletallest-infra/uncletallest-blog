Everyone has opinions about tech stacks. Most of those opinions are about performance benchmarks, ecosystem size, or whatever the last conference talk was about.

None of that is wrong. But for those of us whose brains work a little differently, there's a question that comes before all of that:

**Will I still be able to work in this when I come back to it after two weeks away?**

That's the real filter.
